        <h1>About atelier&nbsp;alt.formula</h1>
        <p>
          atelier alt.formula is a creative studio working across art, textile,
          space, and craft, reconsidering the relationships between material and
          structure, body and space. Moving beyond the boundaries of art and
          design, we examine, deconstruct, and reconstruct existing formats and
          aesthetics through experimental processes and material research.
        </p>
        <p>
          We understand “form” not as a fixed and completed shape, but as a
          “formula” — a generative system through which forms continuously
          emerge and transform. Through this perspective, we explore
          alternative modes of creation and redefine the essence of things,
          their relationships, and the structures that shape them.
        </p>
        <h2>Services</h2>
        <ul>
          <li>
            <strong>Concept Development &amp; Research:</strong> We conduct
            research into materials, culture, space, structure, and the body,
            developing concepts that can be translated into exhibitions,
            products, spatial expressions, and artistic projects.
          </li>
          <li>
            <strong>Textile &amp; Material Development:</strong> Through dyeing,
            surface treatment, textile experimentation, and material
            processing, we develop original material expressions for spaces,
            objects, and products.
          </li>
          <li>
            <strong>Spatial &amp; Exhibition Design:</strong> We design
            exhibitions, installations, display structures, circulation, and
            spatial compositions by considering the relationship between
            materials, objects, light, and the body.
          </li>
          <li>
            <strong>Product &amp; Object Production:</strong> We plan and
            produce furniture, objects, products, and art pieces based on the
            structure, texture, and behavior of materials.
          </li>
          <li>
            <strong>Creative Direction:</strong> We shape the overall concept,
            visual language, editorial direction, exhibition structure,
            photography, video, and graphic direction of a project, designing a
            coherent world and experience across all outputs.
          </li>
          <li>
            <strong>Archive &amp; Documentation:</strong> We document and edit
            production processes, material experiments, fieldwork, and
            exhibitions through photography, video, text, and research
            mapping.
          </li>
          <li>
            <strong>Collaboration &amp; Custom Production:</strong> We
            collaborate with brands, architects, artists, and cultural projects
            to develop custom materials, objects, installations, and
            research-based spatial expressions.
          </li>
        </ul>

        <p>
          Our creation begins not from a finished form, but from observing
          materials, places, techniques, bodies, and memories. Through
          fieldwork we visit various sites to read the contexts embedded in
          each place – culture, industry, landscape, history and craft. We
          document how materials are born, used, transformed, and come to
          hold traces of memory.
